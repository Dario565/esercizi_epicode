Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holyday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes thath go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpouse flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
